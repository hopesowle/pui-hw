About
Hope Sowle

S E N D M E A N E M A I L

Inspired by her great-grandfather's photographic work within the United States Navy, Hope's fascination with photography ignited at an early age. This passion continued into her undergraduate studies at Carnegie Mellon University, where she pursues a degree in cognitive science, complemented by a minor in photography. Hope's unique perspective, shaped by her studies of the mind, intricately weaves into the sequencing and composition of her work.

Currently, Hope is immersed in the cyclical nature and unwavering dedication of commercial fishermen. Through her lens, she seeks to unveil the profound stories woven into their lifestyle, capturing the beauty and commitment intrinsic to their world.
